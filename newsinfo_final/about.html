STARTUP IN INDIA

Startup India and stand up India are both schemes by the Government to promote better economic growth and development. The systems allow people to do entrepreneurship. Startup India promotes initiatives and new ventures while standing up India promotes entrepreneurship among the scheduled cast and scheduled tribe women.The initiative is vital and beneficial for the youth in both rural and urban areas. Entrepreneurship has become a very successful and widely respected profession in the past few years.
